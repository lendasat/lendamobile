# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 5, 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Lendasat Wallet mobile application ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to our servers:

- **Wallet Data**: Private keys, seed phrases, and wallet addresses are stored locally and encrypted on your device. We never have access to this information.
- **Transaction History**: Your transaction history is stored locally on your device.
- **App Preferences**: Settings and preferences you configure within the App.

### Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

- **Camera**: Used solely for scanning QR codes (e.g., Bitcoin addresses, Lightning invoices, payment requests). Images are processed locally and are not stored or transmitted.
- **Biometric Authentication**: Used to secure access to your wallet. Biometric data is handled by your device's operating system and is never accessed by the App.
- **Internet**: Required to broadcast transactions and sync with the Bitcoin/Ark network.

### Analytics Data

We use PostHog for anonymous usage analytics to improve the App. This may include:

- App version and device type
- Feature usage patterns
- Crash reports

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you or your wallet.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Private keys or seed phrases
- Wallet balances or transaction details
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Contact information

## Data Security

- All sensitive wallet data is encrypted and stored locally on your device
- We use industry-standard security practices
- The App is open source and can be audited

## Third-Party Services

The App interacts with:

- **Bitcoin/Lightning/Ark Networks**: To broadcast and receive transactions
- **PostHog**: For anonymous analytics (can be disabled in settings)

## Your Rights

You can:

- Delete all App data by uninstalling the App
- Backup your wallet data at any time

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.

## Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
